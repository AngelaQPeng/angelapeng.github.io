Tool(s):

External Sources: SketchFab, Mixamo, freesound.org
Partner: Andy (Yuhan) Yang
Project Length: 3 Weeks

Project Overview

A location-based augmented reality mobile game inspired by "Pokemon Go" but tailored to the environment and culture of Ann Arbor (A2), Michigan. In this game, players venture throughout A2 to explore its landmarks, historical sites, and iconic locations, all while interacting with various virtual elements in the game.
