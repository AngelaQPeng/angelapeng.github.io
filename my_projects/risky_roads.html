Project Overview

This application is dedicated to simulating the consequences of hazardous driving scenarios. Currently, our focus lies on two critical situations: texting while driving and driving under the influence. Through this initiative, our aim is to heighten awareness surrounding these perilous situations and deter reckless behavior on the road.

The motivation for our driving simulation platform arises from a significant concern surrounding the frequency of traffic fatalities resulting from reckless driving. In the initial half of 2021 alone, an alarming 20,160 lives were lost in road accidents. Furthermore, a daily average of 37 fatalities is attributed to incidents involving drunk driving.

This disconcerting trend propels our dedicated commitment to the development of this simulation platform. With a dual emphasis on education and research, our overarching objective is to make a substantive contribution to collective efforts aimed at mitigating road-related fatalities and elevating overall road safety standards.
